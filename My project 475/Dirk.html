HALL OF FAME
Dirk Nowitzki announced that he was retiring from the game of basketball after spending 21 years with the Dallas Mavericks. Nowitzki finishes his career with 1 NBA finals Title, 6th on the all time NBA scoring list, 14-time All NBA honors, and 12-time NBA allstar apperances. It is very evident that the game of basketball will not be the same without Nowitzki. He was the first '7 who really had his presence felt at the three point line. Dirk opened the window for many young 7 foot players. On April 9,2019 the Dallas Mavericks gave Nowitzki a final tribute during his final home game. Nowitzki left his mark during his last home game as he is now the oldest player (41 years 274 days) to score 30+points in an NBA game. Dirk will be dearly missed.
